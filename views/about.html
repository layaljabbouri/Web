About CentraleSupélec

Overview

CentraleSupélec is one of the leading engineering schools in France, renowned for its excellence in education, research, and innovation. With a rich history spanning over several decades, CentraleSupélec has established itself as a key player in the global engineering landscape.

History

CentraleSupélec was formed in 2015 through the merger of École Centrale Paris and Supélec, two prestigious engineering institutions with a long-standing tradition of academic excellence. This merger brought together the strengths and expertise of both institutions to create a unified powerhouse in engineering education and research.

Academic Programs

CentraleSupélec offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, and more. The programs are designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of engineering principles and prepare them for successful careers in their chosen fields.

Research and Innovation

Research and innovation are at the heart of CentraleSupélec's mission. The school is home to world-class research centers and laboratories, where faculty and students collaborate on cutting-edge projects spanning multiple disciplines. Through its research endeavors, CentraleSupélec aims to address some of the most pressing challenges facing society and contribute to the advancement of knowledge and technology.

Community

CentraleSupélec boasts a vibrant and diverse community of students, faculty, and staff from across the globe. The school prides itself on fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment, where creativity, collaboration, and innovation thrive. Through various extracurricular activities, clubs, and events, students have ample opportunities to engage with their peers, pursue their passions, and make lasting connections.
